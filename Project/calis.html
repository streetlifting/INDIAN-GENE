Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a skill sport that consists of many bodyweight skills like: the Muscel Up,Planche,Pull ups,Dip etc. The goal is to master your bodyweight and to built beast like stamina which help to build muscel and improve movements.

Why Calisthenics?

  • Develops control over your body,moving your body like gravity does'nt exist.
  • Improves Joint stability and mobility.
  • Enhances athletic performance in various sports.
  • Boosts cardiovasular and reduce chances of heart disease.

Calisthenics Training Basics

Calisthenics training is structured around reps and sets:

  • Volume-focused workouts (8-12 reps per set).
  • Accessory exercises for weak points.
  • Periodization for long-term progress.
  • Deload weeks to prevent injuries.
